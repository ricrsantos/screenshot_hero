# PRD-002 - Canvas and Navigation

Status: Draft

---

## Objective

Provide an image canvas suitable for annotation workflows.

---

## User Story

As a user,

I want to navigate large screenshots,

so that I can annotate specific areas accurately.

---

## Functional Requirements

### FR-001

Display loaded image.

### FR-002

Support zoom in.

### FR-003

Support zoom out.

### FR-004

Support fit to window.

### FR-005

Support 100% zoom.

### FR-006

Support panning.

### FR-007

Display current zoom level.

### FR-008

Support scroll wheel zoom.

### FR-009

Maintain image quality during zoom.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

✅ Zoom works

✅ Pan works

✅ Large screenshots are usable

✅ Rendering remains responsive
